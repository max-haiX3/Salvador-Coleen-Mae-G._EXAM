Rise From The Dead: Max S. Back From 6
Months Hiatus

By Coleen Mae Salvador

Published Mar 14, 2025 4:00 PM


(File photo)

Max S. also known as Coleen Mae Salvador had went into an unannounced
hiatus precisely on Wednesday, September 18, 2024. Netizens speculated that
it is due to rapid decline on mental health. They have been spotted out and
about on March 11, 2025.

A private interview has been conducted with Max, they said "It was both
calming yet haunting to be away from the outside world," Describing their
experience inside the institution.

The allegations regarding their disappearance has been discussed, Max
mentioned that it is true that their depression has worsen over the past year to
the point of attempting suicide. This is the main reason as to why they are
admitted in the first place however, the reason of attempt remain private.

Family and friends are gladly welcoming their return. As of current events,
they are back on attending school regularly and has been able to keep up with
their studies unlike before.
